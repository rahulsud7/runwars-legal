Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: JUNE 2026

Welcome to RunWars. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the augmented reality fitness experience of RunWars, we must collect specific data points:

  • Geolocation Data: We collect persistent, real-time background location (GPS) data when you are actively running and utilizing the "Tracker" feature. This is required to calculate your distance and determine if you have captured territorial grid hexes.
  • Account Data: We collect your chosen callsign (username), email address, and authentication data via secure third-party providers (Clerk).
  • Health/Fitness Data: We access local pedometer/step-count data on your device to supplement distance tracking during active missions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is utilized strictly for gameplay mechanics and system functionality:

  • To track your physical movement and translate it into in-game points and territory captures.
  • To maintain live, global leaderboards and syndicate (club) rankings.
  • To monitor for cheating, GPS-spoofing, or violation of our terms.

3. Public Data & Multiplayer Elements

RunWars is a competitive multiplayer game. By participating, you acknowledge that certain data will be publicly visible to other players on the tactical map, including: your Username, your Club affiliation, and the geographical territories/hexes you currently control.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal data or location history to third-party advertising networks. Your routing data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers (AWS/Supabase) solely for calculating game state. We may share information with government authorities only if compelled by law.

5. Account Deletion

You may request the complete deletion of your account and associated telemetry data at any time by contacting support or using the deletion protocol within the App settings.

Account & Data Deletion

EFFECTIVE DATE: JUNE 2026

If you no longer wish to participate in RunWars, you have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and all associated location/gameplay data.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

The fastest way to delete your data is directly within the RunWars app:

  • Open the RunWars app and log in.
  • Navigate to the Profile tab on the bottom navigation bar.
  • Tap the settings/gear icon to open your Account Management.
  • Select Delete Account and confirm. Your data will be wiped immediately.
